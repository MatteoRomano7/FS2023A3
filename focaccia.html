Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie.

Wow your tastebuds with this festive focaccia appetizer topper with sweet caramelized onion,
tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!


Image: Sally Vegas


FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberry To Go Beyond Sauce


Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olice oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
